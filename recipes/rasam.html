Rasam

Steps to prepare the recipe:

1. Prepare Tamarind Water: Mix tamarind paste in 2 cups warm water (or squeeze soaked tamarind). Add chopped tomato, turmeric, crushed garlic, salt, pepper, and cumin.

2. Boil the Rasam: Bring the tamarind mix to a gentle boil. Let it simmer for 5–7 minutes until raw smell of tamarind goes. (Optional: Add cooked toor dal now for a thicker rasam and simmer for 2–3 minutes.)

3. Do the Tempering (Tadka): In a small pan, heat 1 tsp oil or ghee. Add mustard seeds, dried red chili, curry leaves. Let them splutter and pour this over the rasam.

4. Garnish & Serve: Add chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice or as a soup.
