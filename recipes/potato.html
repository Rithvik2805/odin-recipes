Potato

Steps to prepare the recipe:

1. Prep the Potatoes: Peel and dice potatoes into small cubes. Rinse and soak in water for 5–10 minutes to remove excess starch (optional).

2. Start the Tempering: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and a pinch of hing (if using).

3. Cook the Potatoes: Drain and add the diced potatoes to the pan. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well so the spices coat the potatoes.

4. Fry Until Crispy: Cook on medium flame, stirring occasionally. Fry until potatoes are golden and crisp (15–20 minutes). Don’t cover the pan, or they’ll turn soft instead of crispy.

5. Serve: Serve hot with rasam rice, sambar, or curd rice.
