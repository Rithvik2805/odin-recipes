Biryani

Process to make tasty biryani:

1. Cook Rice: Wash 1 cup basmati rice and soak for 20 minutes. Boil with salt and a few whole spices (optional) until 80% cooked. Drain and set aside.

2. Cook Chicken Masala: Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste; sauté for a minute. Add chicken and fry for 5 minutes. Add turmeric, chili powder, salt, biryani masala, and chopped tomato. Add yogurt and cook until chicken is done (10–15 mins).

3. Layer Biryani: In a pot, layer half the cooked rice. Add all the chicken masala. Top with remaining rice. Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves (optional). Cover and cook on low flame for 10 minutes (dum cooking).

4. Serve: Gently mix before serving. Serve hot with raita or salad.
